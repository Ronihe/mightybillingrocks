Invoice & Minimums Calculation

DBM

Invoice Amount = ((Media Cost * (1+Tech Fee) + Data Fee)) * (1+Service Fee)

Contribution Towards Minimum = Invoice Amount - (Media Cost + Data Fee)

DCM

Invoice Amount = (CPM or CPC) * DCM Price Scheme * (1+Service Fee)

Contribution Towards Minimum = DCM Invoice Amount

DS

Invoice Amount = (DS Spending) * (1+ DS Price Scheme) * (1+Service Fee) - DS Spending

Contribution Towards Minimum = DS Invoice Amount
